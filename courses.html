AOIT Courses

Programming

Students learn to program in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Students also learn to plan coding projects, build them, and decode errors.

A+

Students learn the inner workings of computers. Afterwards they apply their knowledge to fix hardware issues and get practice by helping to maintain the machines on campus.

Cyber Security

Students learn to protect information the web - a skill that is growing in demand as more of our personal data goes onto the web. Students learn security conecpts to fight off macro viruses and malware.

CISCO Networking

Students learn the process of creating computer networks. To build networks, students develop an understanding in hardware, software, wireless technology, and electrical engineering.
