Waipahu High School's Academy of Information Technology, or AOIT, is dedicated to students interested in being a part of the IT field. In a world relying more and more on technology, jobs in the IT field are expected to demand over half a million new jobs by 2026, faster than every other occupation.

In AOIT, students learn a variety of skills dealing with infromation technology. Classes range from programming, computer mechanics, and networking. For more information, see the courses here.
